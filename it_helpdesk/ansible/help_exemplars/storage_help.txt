Here is an exemplar of how to use the storage management function of the IT-Helpdesk to increase your accounts storage allocation, or to query your account to see how much space you have left/how much you have used:

Increasing Storage:

Subject: Increase Storage Quota
Body:
Increase the storage allocation for user: [your_username] [by/to] [desired amount] GB.

Querying account storage:
Subject: Storage Query
Body:
How much storage does user: [your_username] have left
